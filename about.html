Udyam 2015

This fest is the annual techno-management fest of the Department of Electronics Engineering. It is organized by the Electronics Engineering Society (EES), IIT (BHU). This fest covers the events ranging from programming to hardware to management. The main motive of the fest is to construct a bridge which can connect our classroom theory and the industrial application, and can give us the opportunity to apply, practically, our knowledge which we have gained through books and in classroom.
